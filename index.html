About the Story

Inspired by experiences growing up in Northern Mexico and living in New York, this story celebrates family traditions, museums, outdoor play, holidays, and everyday joy.

Children explore the beauty of the four seasons through everyday family moments. This heartwarming story supports early language development, emotional connection, cultural identity, and family values.

  • Gardening with grandparents
  • Splashing in the pool
  • Baking cookies & flying kites

Meet the Author

Odila is a bilingual author inspired by her experiences growing up in Northern Mexico, living in Spain, and building a career on the East Coast of the United States.

Her multicultural background and passion for creativity, education, and storytelling strongly influence her work. She believes books are bridges between languages, cultures, and generations.

Her children's books aim to encourage cultural identity, promote emotional growth, and create meaningful family reading moments.
