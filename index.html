About the Story

Inspired by experiences growing up in Mexicali and living in NYC, this story celebrates family traditions, museums, outdoor play, holidays, and everyday joy. Children explore the beauty of the four seasons through everyday family moments. This heartwarming story supports early language development, emotional connection, cultural identity, and family values.

Meet the Author

Odila A. G. Gallego

Odila is a bilingual author inspired by her experiences growing up in Mexicali, living in Majorca, and building a career in NYC.

Her multicultural background and passion for creativity, education, and storytelling strongly influence her work. She believes books are bridges between languages, cultures, and generations.
